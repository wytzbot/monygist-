About MonyGIST

MonyGIST helps regular people start profitable businesses using real US government data, not hype.

🎯 Our Mission

90% of new businesses fail because people start with bad ideas. We fix that. We analyze data from the US Census Bureau, SBA, BLS, and Statista to show you exactly: how much it costs to start, how much you can make, and what mistakes to avoid.

Our goal: Help 1,000,000 people launch their first $10K/month business by 2030.
