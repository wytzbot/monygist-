Editorial Policy & Methodology

MonyGIST is committed to transparency and accuracy.

How We Research

  1. Data Collection: We pull from US Census Bureau, SBA, BLS, Statista 2026
  2. Analysis: Founder Ilemobayo Tolulope analyzes profit margins, startup costs, and competition
  3. Fact-Checking: Every number is cross-referenced with 2+ sources
  4. Updates: Business data is reviewed every 6 months

Independence

MonyGIST does not accept payment to feature businesses. Rankings are based only on data, not sponsorships.

Corrections

Found an error? Email support@monygist.com. We correct mistakes within 48 hours.
